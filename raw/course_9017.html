Remark
Teaching goal Students should develop a deeper understanding of how cities work and interact with the community. How rivalries and hierarchies between cities work and what factors are considered for developing a successful urban economy. Global Network Theory, the function of tolerance (R. Florida) and modern ITTechnologies (M. Castells) are key theories that contribute to this understanding
