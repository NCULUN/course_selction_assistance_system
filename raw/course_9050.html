Teaching goal To understand the interplay between science and technology through history
Teaching content 1. Introduction
2. Pre-Newtonian science
3. From industrial revolution to the atomic structure
4. From the structure of DNA to the central dogma of molecular biology
5. From quantum mechanics to materials sciences
6. The rise of complex systems science
7. From mathematics to information science
