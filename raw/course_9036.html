Remark
Teaching goal “First, the teaching objectives: 1. To encourage students to understand how to interpret the status of communication and media content. 2. To help students develop knowledge and insight into the nature of media criticism. 3. through values clarification process, so that the students can be near, analyze, evaluate a variety of media information, text production system, and then pass the message to express ideas and achieve the purpose of communication.“
